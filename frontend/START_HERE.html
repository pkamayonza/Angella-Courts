🚀 Quick Setup

⚠️ Required Setup

You must set up Formspree for forms to work. Go to formspree.io, create two forms (booking & contact), and update the form IDs in pages/booking.html and pages/contact.html

Step 1: Get Formspree ID

Visit formspree.io, create two forms, and copy the IDs

Step 2: Update Form IDs

Open pages/booking.html and pages/contact.html, replace YOUR_FORMSPREE_ID

Step 3: Test Locally

Open index.html in your browser and test all pages and forms

Step 4: Deploy

Upload the frontend folder to any static hosting (Netlify, Vercel, traditional hosting)
