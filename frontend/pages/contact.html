Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our accommodation

When can I move in?

▼

Move-in dates depend on the semester. For Semester 1 2026, move-in begins in late July/early August. For Semester 2 2027, move-in is in late January/early February. Exact dates will be confirmed when you submit your booking request and early move-in may be available upon request.

Is electricity included in the rent?

▼

Yes! Electricity is fully included in your semester fee. We maintain stable power supply throughout the hostel compound and there are no additional electricity charges.

Can I pay in installments?

▼

Yes, we offer flexible payment options, you can pay the full semester upfront, or split payments across the semester (typically in 2-3 installments). Payment terms will be discussed with our caretaker when your booking is confirmed.

Is there parking available?

▼

Yes, we have secure parking available for students with motorbikes and bicycles. For cars, limited parking is available on a first-come, first-served basis at a small additional fee. Contact us for parking details and rates.

Are meals provided?

▼

Meals are not included in the room fee. However, the hostel offers good access to local restaurants and food vendors near Kabale University. Many students prepare their own meals or take advantage of the nearby food establishments. Kitchenettes are not available in rooms, but you can arrange access to designated cooking areas.
